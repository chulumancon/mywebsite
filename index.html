 what is Shwe Shwe

Shwe Shwe is a printed dyed cotton fabric that is typically used for traditianal  Southern African clothing.The fabric is is manufactured in a different  colours  and printing designs. Shwe SHWe is distinguished by the inticate geometric patterns.Shwe Shwe is commonly reffered to as the denim of South Africa.
